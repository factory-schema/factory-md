# Sunrise Textiles Pvt. Ltd.

## Summary

Vertically integrated knit garment manufacturer in Tirupur, India, specializing in organic cotton basics and activewear for global retail and private label brands. Founded 1997, 500–600 employees, GOTS and GRS certified, with on-site knitting, dyeing, cutting, sewing, and finishing.

## Capabilities

Knitting, dyeing, cut-and-sew, embroidery, screen printing, sublimation printing, garment washing.

**Finishes:** enzyme wash, silicone softener, anti-pilling, moisture wicking, wrinkle-free, UV protection, anti-microbial.

**Fabric types:** jersey, interlock, rib, pique, french terry, fleece. GSM range 120–400.

**Garment types:** t-shirts, polo shirts, hoodies, joggers, leggings, tank tops. Size range XS–5XL. Color matching: Pantone TPX/TCX.

**Secondary services:** pattern making/grading, labeling and tagging, hang tags and packaging, poly bagging, carton packing, retail-ready packaging.

**Tolerances:**
- Standard: ±0.5 cm (body measurements per size spec)
- By process: cutting ±0.3 cm, sewing (seam placement) ±0.2 cm, print placement ±0.3 cm, embroidery placement ±0.2 cm
- Min feature: 5 mm embroidery detail

**Capacity & constraints:** MOQ 500–50,000 pieces. Capacity 50,000 pieces/month. Min order value $2,000 USD.

**Engineering / pre-production services:** DFM review, prototyping, reverse engineering, co-design. Accepted files: AI (Adobe Illustrator), PDF, DXF, CDR (CorelDRAW), tech pack (Excel/PDF). In-house CAD: Gerber AccuMark, Optitex, Adobe Illustrator.

## Quality

AQL 2.5 final inspection, inline QC at cutting and sewing, pre-shipment lab test report. Dedicated QC team of 15 inspectors. Defect rate: 2,500 PPM. GOTS-compliant chain of custody from fiber to finished garment — each lot linked to organic cotton source and dyeing batch records.

**Testing:** fabric GSM, color fastness (wash, rub, light — ISO 105 series), shrinkage (AATCC 135), pilling resistance (ISO 12945), dimensional stability, needle detection, tensile/burst strength.
